About Me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Mathematics at ETH Zurich. My research interests lie at the intersection of probability theory and deep learning, with a focus on developing mathematical foundations for deep learning algorithms.

Research

My current research is focused on developing new theoretical frameworks for analyzing the performance of deep learning algorithms, with a particular emphasis on their probabilistic properties.
